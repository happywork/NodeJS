About our company

And its goals

Marine-Chain Platform is the next generation of the global vessel market which is possible due to the blockchain.

Marine-Chain Platform aims to redesign the traditional vessel market, lower its entry barriers and help vessel investors to diversify their portfolio. Further, our platform finally brings liquidity to a very thin vessel market.

Transferring physical vessels into digital assets

Such an event is called tokenization of the vessel and is where any ship owner can issue a fundraising campaign and raise a Vessel Token Offering of a single vessel or a fleet of them. Those tokens are ERC20 compliant, which can be exchanged and freely traded with anyone on Marine-Chain Exchange or any other trustless P-2-P (peer-to-peer) exchanges.

Project advantages

Why Marine-Chain?

Trustless & Global Reaching

Tokenization of the vessel and trading under free market conditions allows transferring ownership in entirely trustless P-2-P exchanges. These exchanges further allow for anyone, anywhere in the world, to participate in the vessel market. Current market conditions only allow vessel investment in certain jurisdictions.

Simple & Convenient

Co-ownership of the single vessel tends to be a tedious task from legal standpoint. This further prohibits a mass co-ownership of the given vessel. A simplified process is built to ensure that this is no longer the case. The amount of paperwork and administration is drastically reduced too.

Liquid & Diversified

It is no longer a problem to buy or sell a vessel as digital asset holders will earn rights to the physical assets: vessels. Before Marine-Chain the only possibility to invest in the vessels market was to either buy the whole vessel or find co-owners willing to split the price & costs. This is no longer the case, as it is now possible to not only diversify the portfolio when investing in several vessels rather than just one, but also hold vessels as cryptoassets.
